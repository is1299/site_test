Multimodal AI combining EHR and CT imaging to predict cardiovascular disease risk in breast cancer patients.

Abstract

We present a multimodal artificial intelligence model that integrates electronic health records and computed tomography scans to predict cardiovascular disease risk in breast cancer patients. The approach leverages complementary clinical and imaging signals to improve risk stratification and support patient-specific decision-making.
